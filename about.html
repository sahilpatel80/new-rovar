Our Story

Creating Better Guest Experiences Since 2006.

Hospitality-focused amenities created around comfort, presentation and practical guest needs.

Our Heritage

Comfort Begins with the Smallest Details.

ROVAR is a manufacturer of hotel guest amenities focused on creating products that support comfort, hygiene and memorable hospitality experiences.

We understand that even the smallest details can shape how a guest remembers their stay. That is why our portfolio brings together bath essentials, personal care products and practical guest room accessories designed around the needs of modern hospitality.

From boutique properties to larger hospitality businesses, we work to provide dependable product quality, flexible solutions and professional service.

Every stay begins with a sense of comfort.

At ROVAR, we believe thoughtful amenities contribute to that feeling through the details guests see, touch and use throughout their stay.

Our Mission

To help hospitality businesses create better guest experiences.

We achieve this through dependable, thoughtfully designed and customizable B2B amenity solutions tailored around client identities.
