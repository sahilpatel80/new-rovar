Hotel Slippers Designed for Guest Comfort.

A complete selection of comfort-focused slippers crafted for luxury suites, wellness spas, and guest bedrooms.

Our Slippers Portfolio

Materials Selected for Guest Walk Comfort

ROVAR manufactures slippers in multiple material finishes, styles, and soles to suit different hospitality tiers and climate profiles.

Non-Woven Economy

Economy slippers in lightweight fabrics, suitable for short stays, quick dry bath use, and budget-conscious standard rooms.

Soft Terry Towel

Comfortable towel fabric slippers, perfect for spa facilities, bath suite lounges, and upscale boutique rooms.

Fleece & Polar

Premium thick fleece slippers designed to keep feet warm and comfortable during winter seasons and luxury suites.

Jute & Waffle Weave

Differentiated texture slippers combining rustic jute outers or traditional waffle weaves for a unique, organic room theme.

Sole Specifications

Comfort Options for Different Hospitality Needs

Selected variants may be available with different sole thickness and construction options depending on product specification.

Outsole Thicknesses

Slippers can be customized with 2mm, 3mm, 4mm, or 5mm EVA non-slip soles, providing structural support and slip safety on tiled bath floors.

Open-Toe vs. Closed-Toe

Select between open-toe designs (perfect for warm climates and spa usage) and closed-toe designs (providing added warmth in cold seasons).
