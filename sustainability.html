Considered Hospitality

Thoughtful Choices for Modern Hospitality.

Hospitality is evolving, and guest expectations are changing with it. ROVAR continues to explore product and material options that help properties make more considered choices without compromising practicality and presentation.

Our Initiatives

Considered Material & Packaging Directions

We explore options that allow hotel procurement groups to select packaging formats and material types aligned with their property directions.

Direction 01

Eco-Conscious Alternatives

Selected guest room accessories crafted using paper packaging wrappers, cardboard boxes, and wood handles instead of traditional single-use plastics.

Direction 02

Reusable Options

Refillable pump dispenser bottles and bulk refill liquid containers that help properties reduce small plastic bottle wastage in daily room setups.

Direction 03

Selected Compostable Material Options

A selection of guest care items utilizing plant-starch and compostable wraps. Available options depend on confirmed order specifications and packaging designs.

Direction 04

Applicable BPA-Free Options

Amenity packaging formats manufactured to comply with BPA-free standards, ensuring safe hygiene standards for guests across personal care bottles.

B2B Material Disclosures

Product Specificity and Compliance

At ROVAR, we emphasize factual product disclosures. Because materials behave differently under different manufacturing and storage conditions, specifications vary by product selection and order configurations.

Selected products in our portfolio may use eco-conscious materials depending on order configurations. Please confirm material data, composition percentages, and packaging test data with our team before order finalization.

Availability and material specifications may vary by product. Our team can help identify suitable options for specific hospitality requirements.
